Where I'm headed

Transactional sports
and corporate law

My USC capstone looked at regulatory instability in college athletics — NIL rights, the transfer portal, and the case for federal oversight. Writing it taught me something I keep coming back to: legal frameworks decide what deals are possible before anyone gets to the negotiating table.

Court internships at the LA Superior Court and the District Attorney's Office showed me how the system runs day to day across criminal, civil, family, and mental health proceedings. The 450 hours I put in with College Corps on hunger relief across LA County are the other half of it. I'd like to spend a career closing deals and still be useful to the communities I came from.
